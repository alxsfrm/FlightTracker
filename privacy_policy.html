About Flight Tracker

Flight Tracker is a mobile application developed by Yossi Levi, based in Israel. The App allows users to track flights and provides map functionality, with a free version supported by ads and an optional in-app purchase to remove ads.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

The App does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you contact us directly. However, we may collect the following data:

  • Location Data: If you enable location services, the App accesses your approximate or precise location to provide map functionality (e.g., showing your position or nearby airports) via Google Maps. This data is used in real-time and is not stored by the App. You can enable or disable location access at any time through your device settings.
  • Device Information: We collect device identifiers (e.g., device ID, IP address) and other diagnostic data through Google Mobile Ads to provide personalized advertising in the free version.
  • Purchase Data: If you purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app feature, we collect purchase history and transaction details, processed securely by Google Play.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you interact with the App (e.g., pages visited, features used) to monitor and improve the App’s performance.

How We Collect Data

  • Automatically: Device Information and Usage Data are collected automatically when you use the App, such as your device’s IP address, operating system, and interaction patterns.
  • With Permission: Location Data is collected only with your explicit permission via Android location settings.
  • Through Third Parties: Purchase Data is collected via Google Play’s billing system.

Use of Your Personal Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the App: Location Data enables map functionality; Device Information supports ad delivery; Purchase Data facilitates the "Remove Ads" feature.
  • To Improve the App: Usage Data helps us analyze trends and enhance features.
  • To Process Transactions: Purchase Data ensures successful in-app purchases via Google Play.
  • To Comply with Legal Obligations: We may use data to meet legal requirements or resolve disputes.

Sharing Your Personal Data

We share your data only in the following cases:

  • With Service Providers: We share data with Google for:
    • Google Maps (Location Data for map functionality).
    • Google Mobile Ads (Device Information for personalized ads).
    • Google Play (Purchase Data for in-app purchases).
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.
  • With Your Consent: We share data for other purposes only with your explicit consent.

We do not share your data with other third parties unless specified above or required by law.

Retention of Your Personal Data

  • Location Data: Not stored; used only in real-time for map functionality.
  • Device Information: Retained by Google Mobile Ads as needed for ad services, subject to Google’s policies.
  • Purchase Data: Retained by Google Play as needed for transaction records, subject to Google’s policies.

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or to comply with legal obligations.
